Bill Withers, may he rest in peace, was known for his amazing live performances

Bill Withers spent much of the 70s and early 80s creating some of the most timeless, heartfelt music in the American canon.
During live performances, his entire band sat down as if they were in the living room of the audience, creating an intimacy
that was rare for such a high profile performer. Songs like Lean On me, Grandma's Hands, Use me and Lovely Day will
forever live in the hearts. and minds of music lovers around the world.
